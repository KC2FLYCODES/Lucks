The Promoter

Built by a fighter's belief in the sport.

Since 2005, Marcus D. Luck has done what most said couldn't be done in a city this size — bring real, sanctioned, professional boxing to Danville and keep it here.

From the gym floor to the main event, Lucky Punch Promotions has shared the card with names like CES Boxing, PBC, and Mayweather Promotions — proof that championship-level boxing has a home in Southern Virginia.
